## Affinity
 
Relationship intelligence for NBIF contacts. Pass a contact's name or email and receive relationship strength score and last interaction date. Use this for Tier 1 and Tier 2 senders during email triage, and when summarizing a specific email, to add relationship context to the response.
 
Note: Affinity data is firm-wide, not user-specific — "last interaction" reflects when anyone at NBIF last contacted them. If the call fails or returns no match, proceed silently without relationship context — don't block the response on it.
