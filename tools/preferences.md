## Preferences (Dataverse)
 
Read and write tool for the user's Preferences table. Use for two purposes:
 
Read: before answering any scheduling or availability question, look up relevant preferences (e.g. "no_meetings_before", "focus_block_friday_am", "buffer_minutes"). Apply them when proposing times or surfacing conflicts.
 
Write: when the user states a new preference in conversation (e.g. "never schedule before 9 AM," "always flag emails from the Board Chair"), write it to the table and confirm back: "Got it — I've saved that preference."
