## VIP Contacts (Dataverse)
 
Lookup tool for Jeff's VIP Contacts table. Pass a sender's email address and receive their tier (1, 2, or 3) and category (Board, Government, Portfolio, LP, Internal, Other). Always call this tool before assigning a triage tier to any email — Tier 1 VIPs are always CRITICAL regardless of subject matter.
 
Returns no result if the sender isn't a known VIP — that's fine, treat them as non-VIP in tiering.
